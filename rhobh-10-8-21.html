'RHOBH' Season 12 Cast and Filming Schedule Revealed

 Posted on October 9, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  130 words  |  Larita Shotwell

During this season of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, Garcelle Beauvais and Sutton Stracke have been asking the hard questions.

The two women were the first to admit that they didn’t trust Erika Jayne‘s stories about Tom Girardi, and although the other women have tried to force them to back down, they refused. Eventually, they started to get the ladies on their side, which is all about to come to a head at the upcoming reunion specials.

If you’re an avid watcher of the franchise, you know they usually film during the wintertime, which is coming up! Bravo plans to jump right back into filming after the reunion airs in order to pick up with the women right where they left off.

But will everyone want to return?
