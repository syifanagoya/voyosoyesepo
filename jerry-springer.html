About

American Talk Show Host Jerry Springer was born Gerald Norman Springer on 13th February, 1944 in Highgate, London, England, UK and passed away on 27th Apr 2023 Evanston, Illinois, USA aged 79. He is most remembered for serving as the 56th Mayor of Cincinnati from 1977 to 1978, and for hosting his mega-popular but sometimes controversial long-running, eponymous syndicated tabloid talk show Jerry Springer from 1991 to 2018 and the syndicated arbitration-based reality court show Judge Jerry from 2019 until 2022. Gerald Norman Springer (February 13, 1944 – April 27, 2023) was an American broadcaster, journalist, actor, producer, lawyer, and politician. Born in London during World War II to German-Jewish refugees escaping the Holocaust, Springer was raised in Queens, New York City. He attended Northwestern University School of Law, qualified as a lawyer, and first became actively involved in politics working for the campaign of Robert Kennedy in 1968.
