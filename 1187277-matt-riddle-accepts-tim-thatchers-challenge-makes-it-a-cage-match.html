Matt Riddle Accepts Tim Thatchers Challenge, Makes It A Cage Match

 Posted on July 17, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  188 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The feud between Matt Riddle and Timothy Thatcher has grown very quickly, and after Thatcher attacked Riddle last week, the two will now be seeing each other one-on-one in a very special setting.

After making a brief appearance on camera, Riddle called Thatcher a “putz” and said he still wasn’t happy about not only losing his NXT Tag Team Championships, but getting attacked by Thatcher. However, Riddle said that he had a solution to that, and it was to see Thatcher in a scenario that they’re both very familiar with: a cage. Later in the night, NXT confirmed that the two would be taking each on in a Cage Match next week on NXT.
